Glossary
Veloci vocabulary — hover any underlined term in the app to see these definitions inline.
Margin
Income minus all commitments. The rate you have available to save or spend freely. Turns red when negative.
Projection
The expected margin rate at any point in time, calculated by amortizing all known commitments and income. Always a smooth curve — no sudden changes.
Pulse
The selected period on the Horizon graph. The Stack and Summary reflect this period. Click any candle to move the Pulse. Defaults to the most recent closed period.
Horizon
The candlestick chart showing actual margin vs projection over time, extending into the future. Drag to scroll through history.
Drift
How far actual margin deviated from projection in a period. +$X = ahead (good). −$X = behind. Each Stack row shows its contribution to total drift.
Hit
An unexpected cost that pushed a commitment above its projected rate. Car repairs, higher utilities, medical bills. Tagged on Stack rows and on the Horizon candle.
Boost
An unexpected positive — a commitment cost less than projected, or income exceeded expectation. Tax refunds, variable expenses under budget.
Stack
Waterfall breakdown of the selected Pulse period. Each commitment row shows its actual rate, projected rate, and drift contribution. Collapses to save space.
Amortization
How Veloci spreads known future events (like an annual insurance payment) evenly over time so they appear as a stable daily/monthly rate rather than a single spike.
